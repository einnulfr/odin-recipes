Grilled Cheese

This will explain how to make a grilled cheese and how easy it is, and how it's really hard to fail at it and even a child could make it. Like, a very young, not-even-talking-age child could cook this successfully. Even if this child had a disability they could still make this.

Ingredients

  • Bread of your choice (2 per sandwich, unless you're making some kind of big mac like sandwich)
  • Butter
  • Cheese (your choice, more variety is always welcome)
  1. Heat a pan with some butter in it to medium heat
  2. throw your bread in the toaster on low just to get a light crunch
  3. Generously butter your lightly toasted bread on the outside surfaces of the sandwich
  4. cut your cheeses to a consistent-ish thickness and layer on an unbuttered side of bread
  5. Once the pan is to temp, put the loaded half of your sandwich in, buttered side down
  6. Place other slice of buttered bread on top of sandwich, buttered side up
  7. let cook for a minute or two, then carefully flip sandwich
  8. Once cheese is melted through, you're ready to eat! Just make sure to not burn the sandwich in the process
