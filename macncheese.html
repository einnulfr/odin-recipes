Mac and Cheese

This is a recipe for macaroni and cheese. As you can see, most of these recipes can be done by a child...

Ingredients

  • Grated cheese (more variety the better)
  • Elbow macaroni
  • butter (A lot)
  • half and half (or whole milk, your choice)
  • flour
  • salt
  • pepper
  • other seasonings
  1. start cooking your macaroni noodles
  2. make a roux with the butter and flour
  3. add a couple cups of milk to the roux, allow to thicken up, then add more to thin it out
  4. Add cheese slowly to the milk/roux mix
  5. season the hell out of your cheese sauce
  6. Once your noodles are done, pour your cheese sauce mix into the noodles
