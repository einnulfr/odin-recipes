Gumbo

This is a gumbo recipe made from three different types of meat and a ton of seasoning. It's super easy to make in a pressure cooker, however some may enjoy the slow burn of using a giant stock pot to simmer this stuff to death.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb chicken
  • 2 andoulle sausages
  • 1/2 lb shrimp
  • 5 cups chicken stock
  • 1 can crushed tomatoes (28 oz)
  • 1/2 lb of green beans or okra
  • 1 each of green and red bell pepper
  • Parsely (dried or fresh)
  • salt
  • pepper
  • creole seasoning mix
  • oil
  • flour
  1. cut your land animals into bite sized chunks and simmer until seared
  2. cube peppers and throw them in the pot
  3. chop the green beans/okra and throw them in too
  4. Take some flour and oil (about 1/3 cup each) and make a roux
  5. toss in your chicken stock, crushed tomatoes, roux, and season the hell out of it
  6. about 10 minutes before being done, throw in the shrimp
  7. about 3 minutes before being done (and that's up to you), add the parsley, finely chopped if fresh
  8. Viola! yer done
